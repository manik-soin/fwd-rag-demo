# Motor Insurance Policy AUTO-001

## 1. Coverage Summary
This comprehensive motor policy covers the insured vehicle for:
- Own damage: up to HKD 400,000 (market value)
- Third-party bodily injury: unlimited liability as per Motor Vehicles Insurance (Third Party Risks) Ordinance
- Third-party property damage: up to HKD 2,000,000
- Personal accident for driver: up to HKD 500,000
- Medical expenses for passengers: up to HKD 50,000 per person
- Windscreen replacement: up to HKD 5,000 (no deductible)

## 2. Exclusions
The following are NOT covered under this policy:
- 2.1 Driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs
- 2.2 Using the vehicle for commercial hire, racing, or rallying
- 2.3 Damage while the vehicle is driven by an unlicensed driver
- 2.4 Mechanical or electrical breakdown not caused by an accident
- 2.5 Wear and tear, depreciation, or gradual deterioration of tyres
- 2.6 Loss of use or consequential loss
- 2.7 Damage occurring outside Hong Kong SAR unless prior approval obtained

## 3. Deductibles
- Standard excess: HKD 3,000 per claim
- Young driver excess (under 25): additional HKD 5,000
- Unnamed driver excess: additional HKD 3,000
- Theft excess: HKD 5,000

## 4. Named Drivers
- Primary: policyholder
- Additional named drivers may be added for HKD 500 per driver per year
- Unnamed drivers are covered but subject to additional excess

## 5. No-Claims Discount (NCD)
- Year 1 (claim-free): 20% discount
- Year 2 (claim-free): 30% discount
- Year 3 (claim-free): 40% discount
- Year 4+ (claim-free): 50% discount (maximum)
- One at-fault claim reduces NCD by two levels
- NCD is non-transferable to other persons

## 6. Premium
- Annual premium: HKD 8,500
- Current NCD: 30% (Year 2)
- Net premium after discount: HKD 5,950
- Payment frequency: Annual

## 7. Roadside Assistance
- 24/7 towing to nearest authorised workshop (up to 50km)
- Emergency fuel delivery
- Flat tyre replacement
- Battery jump-start
- Lockout assistance
