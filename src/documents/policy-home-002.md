# Home Insurance Policy HOME-002

## 1. Coverage Summary
This policy covers the insured residential property for:
- Fire and explosion: up to SGD 800,000
- Theft and attempted theft: up to SGD 100,000
- Water damage (accidental discharge): up to SGD 50,000
- Personal liability: up to SGD 500,000
- Temporary accommodation: up to SGD 20,000 if home is uninhabitable

## 2. Exclusions
The following are NOT covered under this policy:
- 2.1 Flood, storm surge, or tidal wave damage
- 2.2 Subsidence, ground heave, or landslip
- 2.3 Intentional damage by the insured or household members
- 2.4 Damage to swimming pools, fences, or retaining walls
- 2.5 Loss of cash or negotiable instruments exceeding SGD 500
- 2.6 Damage from renovations or construction work on the property
- 2.7 Cyber-related losses or data breaches

## 3. Deductibles
- Standard deductible: SGD 1,000 per claim
- Water damage deductible: SGD 2,500 per claim
- Liability deductible: SGD 500 per claim

## 4. Claims Process
- 4.1 Report claims within 14 days of discovery
- 4.2 Provide original receipts or valuations for contents claims
- 4.3 FWD may request access to the property for inspection
- 4.4 Settlement within 30 business days of claim approval

## 5. Premium
- Annual premium: SGD 2,400
- Payment frequency: Quarterly (SGD 600)
- Multi-policy discount: 15% if combined with auto or life policy

## 6. Special Conditions
- Property must be occupied at least 60% of the year
- Security alarm system required for theft coverage
- Annual property maintenance records must be available on request
