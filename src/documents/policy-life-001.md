# Term Life Insurance Policy LIFE-001

## 1. Coverage Summary
This 20-year level term life policy provides:
- Death benefit: HKD 3,000,000
- Terminal illness benefit: HKD 3,000,000 (payable upon diagnosis of terminal illness with life expectancy under 12 months)
- Accidental death additional benefit: HKD 1,500,000 (total payout HKD 4,500,000 for accidental death)

## 2. Exclusions
The following are NOT covered under this policy:
- 2.1 Suicide within the first 13 months of the policy effective date
- 2.2 Death resulting from participation in criminal activity
- 2.3 Death caused by war or active military service in a conflict zone
- 2.4 Death from pre-existing conditions not disclosed at application
- 2.5 Death resulting from illegal drug use
- 2.6 Death from extreme sports (base jumping, free solo climbing) unless declared and extra premium paid

## 3. Beneficiaries
- Primary beneficiary: as designated by the policyholder
- Contingent beneficiary: as designated by the policyholder
- If no beneficiary designated, proceeds paid to the estate

## 4. Premium
- Annual premium: HKD 6,800
- Premium remains level for the full 20-year term
- Payment frequency: Monthly (HKD 567)
- Grace period: 31 days for missed payments
- Policy lapses after 31 days of non-payment but may be reinstated within 2 years subject to evidence of insurability

## 5. Conversion Privilege
- This term policy may be converted to a whole life or endowment policy without medical underwriting
- Conversion must be exercised before age 65 or within the first 15 years, whichever is earlier
- Premium for the converted policy will be based on the attained age at conversion

## 6. Claims Process
- 6.1 Notify FWD within 90 days of death
- 6.2 Submit certified death certificate
- 6.3 Submit policy document (or declaration of loss)
- 6.4 Provide identity documents of the beneficiary
- 6.5 FWD will process valid claims within 14 business days of receiving all documents

## 7. Policy Loan
- Not available for term life policies
- Consider converting to whole life if cash value access is needed
