# Home Insurance Policy HOME-001

## 1. Coverage Summary
This policy covers the insured property for the following perils:
- Fire and smoke damage: up to HKD 5,000,000
- Theft and burglary: up to HKD 500,000
- Water damage from burst pipes: up to HKD 200,000
- Windstorm and typhoon damage: up to HKD 1,000,000
- Liability coverage for injuries on property: up to HKD 2,000,000

## 2. Exclusions
The following are NOT covered under this policy:
- 2.1 Flood damage from natural disasters
- 2.2 Earthquake damage
- 2.3 War, terrorism, or civil unrest
- 2.4 Gradual deterioration or wear and tear
- 2.5 Damage caused by pets or domestic animals
- 2.6 Mould or fungal growth unless caused by a covered peril
- 2.7 Loss of jewellery exceeding HKD 50,000 per item unless separately scheduled

## 3. Deductibles
- Standard deductible: HKD 5,000 per claim
- Water damage deductible: HKD 10,000 per claim
- Windstorm deductible: HKD 15,000 per claim

## 4. Claims Process
- 4.1 Claims must be filed within 30 days of the incident
- 4.2 A police report is required for all theft and burglary claims
- 4.3 Photographic evidence of damage must be submitted
- 4.4 FWD reserves the right to appoint an independent assessor

## 5. Premium
- Annual premium: HKD 12,000
- Payment frequency: Monthly (HKD 1,000)
- Late payment grace period: 15 days
- Policy lapses after 30 days of non-payment

## 6. Renewal
- Policy automatically renews unless cancelled 30 days before expiry
- Premium may be adjusted at renewal based on claims history
- No-claims discount: 10% after one claim-free year, up to 30% maximum
